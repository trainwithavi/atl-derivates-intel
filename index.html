Methodology
! This is not a signal tool — the scanner narrows the field. Your chart analysis makes the decision.
01
SCAN THE MARKET
Hit Scan All Pairs. The tool checks 300–500 futures pairs simultaneously — funding rate, OI delta, OI velocity, 4h momentum — and scores every coin.
02
BUILD YOUR WATCHLIST
Review the top coins flagged as PUMP, DUMP, or BUILDING. These are the pairs with derivatives conditions loaded — not random picks. Ignore FIRING and DONE entirely.
03
OPEN THE CHART
Analyse each shortlisted coin independently. Apply your own price action and market structure analysis on the 1H chart — BOS, CHoCH, supply and demand zones, key levels. Do this with zero bias from the scanner.
04
LOOK FOR CONFLUENCE
Only proceed if your chart analysis agrees with the scanner bias. Scanner says PUMP and your chart shows bullish structure at a key demand zone — that is confluence. If they disagree, skip the coin entirely.
05
WAIT FOR YOUR TRIGGER
Drop to your lower timeframe — 15M or 5M. Wait for a structural trigger: CHoCH, FVG fill, order block reaction, liquidity sweep. That is your entry. The scanner told you where to look. Your trigger tells you when.
