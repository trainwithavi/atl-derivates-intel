Confluence Engine

What makes a signal valid

All four gates must open simultaneously. If any gate fails, the system stays flat. Patience is the edge.

GATE 01
Confluence Threshold
Bull or bear score must exceed a minimum % threshold. Adjusts automatically per mode.
GATE 02
Indicator Count
A minimum number of the 15 indicators must agree on direction. No single indicator can dominate.
GATE 03
Relative Strength Margin
Bull score must exceed bear by a configurable margin (default 10%). Eliminates mixed-signal entries.
GATE 04
ADX Trend Filter
ADX must confirm a trend is present. Prevents entries in choppy, directionless markets.
